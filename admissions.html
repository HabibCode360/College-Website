Admission Requirements

What you need to apply to our programs

Undergraduate Programs

Academic Requirements

High school diploma or equivalent with minimum 3.0 GPA

Standardized Tests

SAT (1200+) or ACT (26+) scores required

English Proficiency

TOEFL (80+) or IELTS (6.5+) for international students

Supporting Documents

Personal statement, letters of recommendation, transcripts

Graduate Programs

Academic Requirements

Bachelor's degree from accredited institution with 3.0+ GPA

Standardized Tests

GRE (300+) or GMAT (550+) depending on program

English Proficiency

TOEFL (90+) or IELTS (7.0+) for international students

Supporting Documents

Statement of purpose, CV, letters of recommendation
